Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

SurfProgress ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device: Your name, skill progress, XP, rank, surf sessions, and quiver (surfboard list) are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. We do not upload this data to our servers.

Photos: If you choose to add a photo or video when completing a skill, it is stored on your device only. We use your photo library with your permission to create shareable skill cards. We do not upload your photos to our servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. We do not use analytics or tracking that identifies you.

Sharing

When you use the Share feature, you choose what to share and with whom. Sharing is handled by iOS and goes only to the apps or people you select.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support link or at your preferred contact method.
